Privacy Policy for AppReviewLint

Effective date: April 8, 2026

AppReviewLint is a developer tool used to scan app packages and project files before App Store submission.

1. Data We Process

AppReviewLint may process files you select, such as .ipa, .xcodeproj, and .xcworkspace. Processing is primarily local on your Mac.

2. Personal Data Collection

We do not intentionally collect personal data for advertising. AppReviewLint does not include ad SDKs.

3. Network Access

When you use App Store Connect features, AppReviewLint may connect to Apple services using credentials you provide.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data.

5. Contact

For questions, please use Support.
