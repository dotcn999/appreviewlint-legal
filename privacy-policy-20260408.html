Privacy Policy for AppReviewLint

Effective date: April 8, 2026

AppReviewLint is a developer tool used to scan app packages and project files before App Store submission. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Data We Process

AppReviewLint may process files you select, including .ipa, .xcodeproj, and .xcworkspace. Processing is primarily performed locally on your Mac.

2. Personal Data Collection

We do not intentionally collect personal data for advertising. AppReviewLint does not include third-party ad SDKs.

3. Network Access

When you use App Store Connect related features, AppReviewLint may connect to Apple services using credentials or tokens you provide, only for the requested action.

4. Credentials and Security

Configured credentials are stored locally under your control. You are responsible for protecting your device and account.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Data is not shared with third parties except where required to perform Apple service interactions initiated by you.

6. Data Retention

Reports and local configuration remain on your device until removed by you.

7. Children's Privacy

AppReviewLint is not directed to children.

8. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated over time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, please visit the Support page.
